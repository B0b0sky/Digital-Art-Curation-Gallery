# Digital Art Curation Gallery

A decentralized platform for showcasing digital artwork built on Stacks blockchain that enables artists to exhibit pieces, collectors to acquire authentic art, and curators to validate artistic authenticity.

## Features

- Digital artwork exhibition with detailed descriptions
- Secure art acquisition and ownership transfer
- Curator-based authenticity verification
- Gallery visit tracking and analytics

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions
- \`exhibit-artwork\`: Exhibit a new digital artwork in the gallery
- \`acquire-artwork\`: Acquire ownership of exhibited digital art
- \`verify-artwork\`: Verify artwork authenticity (gallery director only)

### Read-Only Functions
- \`get-artwork\`: Retrieve artwork exhibition details
- \`get-gallery-visit\`: Get specific gallery visit record
- \`get-visit-count\`: Get total gallery visits for an artwork

## Usage

Deploy the contract and interact with it through Clarinet or Stacks CLI to manage digital art curation and sales in a decentralized gallery.
